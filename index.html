Welcome to the Color Scheme Generator!

How It Works:
  • Use the "Add a Color" button to randomly generate a new color.
  • Use the "Clear" button to reset your colors.
  • Hover over a color and click the "X" button to delete that color.
  • Hover over a color and click the "*" button to replace that color.
  • Drag and drop colors to rearrange their order.
  • The color API can sometimes be slow - sorry about that!
